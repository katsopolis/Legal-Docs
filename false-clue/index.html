Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lorewound ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application, False Clue (the "App").

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data from users of this App.

False Clue is designed as an offline-first, local multiplayer game. All game data, including player names, settings, and game states, is stored locally on your device using your device's internal storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third parties.

2. Permissions

The App requires minimal permissions to function properly on your device. We do not request access to sensitive permissions such as Camera, Microphone, Location, or Contacts.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or AdMob). Therefore, no user data is shared with third parties for marketing or analysis purposes.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any data at all, it is safe for users of all ages and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data to servers, there is no risk of your data being intercepted or breached in transit. Your game data resides solely on your personal device. Users can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the App.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
